NCPC-2018, IUBAT

National Collegiate Programming Contest (NCPC-2018) was held on August 3-4, 2018. The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DCSE) of IUBAT–International University of Business Agriculture and Technology..... hosted the contest while ICT Division and Bangladesh Computer Council remained as organisers. IUBAT NCPC-2018 was inaugurated by Zuena Aziz, the Secretary of ICT Division.

In total, 447 contestants of 149 teams from 72 universities, two colleges, and one polytechnic including three teams of female contestants participated in the contest.

Dhaka University's team Epinephrine became champion of NCPC-2018; BUET's team Awww! became first runner-up and Dhaka University's team zuccbot.exe became second runner-up in the contest.

VC of IUBAT Prof. Dr. Abdur Rab presided over the prizegiving ceremony. The event started with welcoming address by the Dean, College of Engineering and Technology, Prof. Dr. Md. Monirul Islam. This was followed by speeches by notable guests like Engr. Enamul Kabir, Director of Bangladesh Computer Council (Training & Development); Prof. Dr. Mohammad Kaykobad, Head of the NCPC-2018 Steering Committee; and special guest Prof. Dr. Falguni Gupta, Regional Contest Director of ACM ICPC (India).

Intra University Programming contest

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DCSE) of IUBAT–International University of Business Agriculture and Technology arrange a Intra University Programming Contest in every semester...... Students from different batches of Department of Computer Science and Engineering (DCSE) participated in this contest.

There are some prizes for the Champion, 1st runnerup and the 2nd runnerup. There are also contest kit for all the participants. The contest take place at the different labs of DCSE.

Freshers Interactive Session

Freshers are the new member of the CSE family. As they are very much new and not familiar with competitive programming, every semester Programmers from the IITS programming club arrange a Interactive session with the fresher students. In this session the members of the IITS programming club discuss about the benifits of programming and motivate them to become a programmer and welcome them to the brains war.
